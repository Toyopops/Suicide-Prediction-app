Important Statistics

Every 40 seconds, someone dies by suicide. It is a significant public health issue that affects millions globally.

In the United States, approximately 45,000 people die by suicide each year.

Seeking help is crucial if you feel overwhelmed. Resources and support systems are available to assist you.

Helpline Numbers

If you are in immediate danger, please call emergency services. In the US, contact the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline at 1-800-273-8255. Trained counselors are available 24/7.

In the UK, you can reach Samaritans at 116 123 for confidential support.

Reaching out to these resources can provide essential support and assistance.
